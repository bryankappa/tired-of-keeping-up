---
name: signal-score
description: Score AI posts and articles for implementation value, workflow relevance, and signal-to-hype ratio.
---

# Purpose
Evaluate whether a normalized item is worth storing, digesting, alerting on, or turning into an experiment.

# Inputs
- title
- body
- author
- source
- url
- engagement metrics
- tags or topics

# Output
- total_score
- verdict
- why_it_matters
- concrete_takeaway
- markdown_entry

# Rules
- Favor implementation detail over popularity.
- Favor benchmark evidence over hot takes.
- Favor ideas that can be tested quickly.
- Penalize repeated or vague content.
- Be concise and strict.
